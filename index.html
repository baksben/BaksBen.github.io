Classification

Predicting patient mortality

I created a predictive model using K-Nearest Neighbor and SVM algorithms, reaching an accuracy of 0.88. Further strategies were used to enhance precision. I dealt with missing data and processed categorical features. Patient data was engineered to improve interpretability. Each variable's significance in my predictive model was evaluated. I tackled class imbalance through oversampling and undersampling, opting for the most effective method. Cross-validation was applied to ascertain my model's best performance.

Forecasting

Forecasting order volumes for Glovo

The project centers around forecasting weekly orders using Glovo data. My approach involved generating features such as days, months, and seasons for analysis. I conducted an EDA to identify trends, noting a daily pattern, and examined outliers for better data comprehension. Finding our data to be stationary, I proceeded to modeling. I utilized the SARIMA model due to its superior handling of seasonality, resulting in highly accurate predictions.

About
